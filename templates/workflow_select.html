About AutoDA

AutoDA is a data automation platform combining structured workflows, NLP automation, and research-ready web scraping. Every step is audited, explainable, and versioned for collaboration.

  • Key capabilities: Auto Mode pipelines, explainable leaderboards, artifact management.
  • Tech stack: Flask, Pandas, scikit-learn, Plotly, NLTK, BeautifulSoup.
  • Use cases: Ops analytics, customer feedback, product research, compliance reporting.
  • Benefits: Faster time-to-insight, reproducible outputs, privacy-aware orchestration.
